Key Design Decisions

  • Panorama section: Tatras photo with background-attachment: fixed (parallax). Semi-transparent dark overlay for card readability.
  • Feature cards: Frosted glass effect (backdrop-filter: blur) on warm-toned cards, horizontally scrollable over the fixed mountain background.
  • On desktop: Cards can be wider and all visible. Horizontal scroll primarily for mobile where space is limited.
  • No navbar: Hero CTA "Szczegóły" smooth-scrolls down. "Zapisz się" jumps to form.
